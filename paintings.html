Painting Stories

Painting is where intuition meets deliberate structure. These works explore luminous color palettes, unexpected architecture, and atmospheric storytelling—capturing the feeling of stepping into a world moments before the scene shifts.

Disciplines Oil & acrylic painting · Digital paintover
Tools Traditional media · Procreate · Krita
Focus Light, scale, and narrative mood

Studio notes

Paintings begin with plein-air thumbnails translated into digital studies. Each final piece blends those sketches with invented architecture to build believable yet dreamlike environments.

Texture passes are intentionally loose, inviting collaboration from writers and sound designers who will extend these moments into larger narrative arcs.
