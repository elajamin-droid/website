Game prototypes are experiments in interaction and mood. Each prototype iterates on mechanic design, level feel, and visual storytelling—all while building documentation so collaborators can expand the experience.

Prototype philosophy

Rapid blockouts evolve into living design documents with embedded gifs, shader breakdowns, and control schemes. I emphasise collaborative iteration—each build ships with notes for programmers, sound designers, and writers.

Current experiments explore tactile sand manipulation, co-op storytelling, and responsive lighting systems that react to player agency.
