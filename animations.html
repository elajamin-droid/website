Cinematic Motion

Motion transforms atmosphere into emotion. These loops blend traditional animation sensibilities with modern pipeline tricks—combining hand-drawn frames, 3D reference, and shader-based texture treatments for cinematic beats ready for film or interactive media.

Disciplines Animation · Motion design · Direction
Tools Toon Boom · After Effects · Unreal Engine
Focus Rhythm, emotion, and cinematography

Animation approach

I build custom timing charts for each loop, allowing subtle slow-ins and snap-outs. Texture overlays are baked through a shader pass that mimics charcoal on glass for a tactile finish.

The collection currently fuels an immersive installation—projected at 180°—where soundscapes shift based on the audience’s proximity to each animated character.
