A process built on play and precision

I map universes first through story, then through light. My methodology bridges concept art, animation, and technical design, making it easy for collaborators to jump in and expand each world.

When I am not painting or developing prototypes, I teach workshops on visual storytelling and consult on art pipelines for indie game studios.

Worldbuilding sprints

Two-week collaborative programs that guide studios from moodboards to shippable concept packs ready for production.

Adaptive pipelines

Custom-built tooling for faster iteration—from shader-based paintovers to procedural animation systems.

Holistic storytelling

Every asset considers music, lore, and gameplay feedback loops so the final world feels cohesive.
