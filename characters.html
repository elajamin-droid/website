Character Universes

Characters begin as whispers of story. Each design explores silhouettes, gesture, and lore-inspired costuming to build worlds that feel inhabited. These studies were guided by mentorship with Joep Eilander (ArtStation) and showcase narrative-driven exploration from sketch to production render.

Disciplines Concept design · Storyboarding · Visual development
Tools Procreate · Photoshop · Blender kitbashes
Focus Worldbuilding through silhouette & costume

Behind the characters

Each persona belongs to a nomadic guild protecting ancient instruments that manipulate weather. Motion studies and costume notes ensure their silhouettes read clearly—even in low light scenes.

The project also includes documentation for gestures, prop functionality, and emotional beat boards to support animators and writers.
